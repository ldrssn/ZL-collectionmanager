# Reclaiming the Narrative

Today marks a shift in how I document my work. The Collection Manager has become a complex, high-fidelity project, but the history was scattered across different machines and accounts.

### Harvesting the Journey
I’ve spent the day "harvesting" the technical documentation—implementation plans, walkthroughs, and task lists—to reconstruct a complete development history. 

### Professional Portfolio Strategy
By splitting our documentation into an **Expert Journal**, a **Narrative Blog**, and a **Strategic Portfolio**, I’m ensuring that this project isn't just a tool, but a professional case study that showcases the design and architecture behind it.

The journey continues, and now it’s properly recorded.
