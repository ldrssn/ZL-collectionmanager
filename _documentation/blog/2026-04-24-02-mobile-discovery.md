# Designing for Mobile Discovery

Following up on this morning’s filter engine, this afternoon was all about **Mobile UX**. How do you manage ten active filters on a small screen?

### The Filter Pill System
I’ve introduced **Filter Pills**. They provide instant visual feedback on exactly what filters are active. More importantly, they allow for "granular dismissal"—you can tap the 'X' on a single pill to broaden your search without resetting everything.

### Horizontal Efficiency
On mobile, these pills now scroll horizontally. This preserves precious vertical space for the collection items themselves. It’s a small refinement that makes the app feel native and high-end on any device.
